High-frequency trading for Digital Assets

Cudera Ltd. is a global market-maker and proprietary trading company specializing in digital assets. We are working with the biggest cryptocurrency exchanges around the world to make both spot and derivative markets more efficient.

About
our mission

Our mission is to increase the efficiency of digital asset markets by providing liquidity and correcting price dislocations. We leverage our knowledge of technology and quantitative trading to create automated strategies that excel in low-latency and optimal risk management.
Technology
We are passionate about working with the programming language Rust for our trading framework. It provides us with safety and speed that guarantees operational risk minimization and fast execution.
Research
We identify opportunities with the tools that best fit the task. We employ simulation, statistical analysis, computational methods and machine learning to improve results

Senior Data Analyst
Cudera's mission is to create a simple global financial...

Cudera's mission is to create a simple global financial infrastructure that empowers billions of people around the world. It's powered by blockchain technology and the plan is to launch it in 2020. Novi is a newly formed Facebook subsidiary whose goal is to provide financial services that will enable people to access and participate in the Libra network. Our first product will be a digital wallet for Libra, a new global currency powered by blockchain technology, and it will be available in 2020 in Messenger, WhatsApp, and as a standalone app. Our vision is to reinvent money and transform the global economy so people everywhere can live better lives. Novi will let you send Libra to almost anyone with a smartphone, as easily and instantly as you might send a text message or photo, and at low to no cost. And, in time, we plan to offer additional services for people and businesses, such as paying bills with the push of a button, buying a cup of coffee with the scan of a code, or riding your local public transit without needing to carry cash or a metro pass.

Responsibilities
  • – Manage data warehouse plans for a product or a group of products
  • – Interface with engineers, product managers and product analysts to understand data needs
  • – Build data expertise and own data quality for allocated areas of ownership
  • – Design, build and launch new data models in production
  • – Design, build and launch new data extraction, transformation and loading processes in production
  • – Support existing processes running in production
  • – Define and manage SLA for all data sets in allocated areas of ownership
  • – Work with data infrastructure to triage infra issues and drive to resolution
Minimum Qualifications
  • – 4+ years of experience with dimensional data modeling & schema design in Data Warehouses
  • – 2+ years of experience in custom or structured (ie. Informatica/Talend/Pentaho)
  • – 2+ years experience with programming languages. Python and Java - advantage
  • – Ability to analyze data to identify deliverables, gaps and inconsistencies
  • – Excellent communication skills including the ability to identify and communicate data driven insights
  • – Ability and interest in managing and communicating data warehouse plans to internal clients
  • – BSc or MS degree in Computer Science or a related technical field
  • – Experience working with either a Map Reduce or an MPP system – an advantage
